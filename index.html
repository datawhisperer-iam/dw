Experience and Awards

Regional Coordinator, Female Leaders Entrepreneurs Conference

Female Leaders & Entrepreneurs Conference (FLEC) is a Gender focused platform designed to encourage women in Management, Leadership, and Business towards self-development and actualization, whilst inspiring learning in the community and promoting sustainability by initiating powerful conversions.

Coordinator, Mastercard Foundation Alumni Network Kenya

The Mastercard Foundation works with visionary organizations to enable young people in Africa and in Indigenous communities in Canada to access dignified and fulfilling work. The Foundation brings together the private sector, donors, young people, and civil society – to create effective systems and infrastructures, supportive policies, and an environment where young people can find work and entrepreneurs and small enterprises can succeed and grow.

Founder, Farmula Limited

Farmula is an agritech startup that automates processes across the agricultural supply chain using farmers' data enabling farmers to sell their products directly to the end consumers. Our business model cuts out irrelevant players across the supply chain enabling farmers to earn up to 25% more and buyers to spend up to 8% less.

Software Developer, Health-e-net

Health-E-Net is a digital health social enterprise based in Nairobi, Kenya. The company combines cutting-edge mobile technologies (e.g browser-based computer vision) with innovative workflows (e.g. maintaining simple, familiar paper-based documentation) and business models (e.g. telemedicine with distributed networks of providers) to enable high-quality healthcare services in resource-limited settings.

Chief Technical Officer, The Gradient Boost

The Gradient boost (TGB) is an online data science boot camp focused on helping students based in Africa build solid foundation skills in data science. TGB supports African students with mentors who are data scientists from various African countries and teaching assistants to give individualized support.
